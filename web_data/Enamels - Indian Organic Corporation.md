###### Enamels

#### Synthetic Enamel Paint

Synthetic enamel paints are based on the alkyds/drying oils which have hard films with gloss retention. The films show excellent brushing properties and little tendency to yellowing on exposure to the environment. The pigment volume concentration controls the gloss, washability, adhesion and durability. The drying may occur at room temperature by oxidation and polymerization. Used on various substrates like wood, metal, train coaches, automobiles industries etc.

#### Stoving Enamel Paint

Stoving enamel paints are similar to synthetic enamel except they cure through thermosetting resins i.e., on application of heat in an oven at 120 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes stoving schedule.

Shades available as per buyer specifications.
