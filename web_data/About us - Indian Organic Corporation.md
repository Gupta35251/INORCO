# About Us

We at Indian Organic Corporation are engaged in trading organic solvents and manufacturing chemical compounded thinners. We also manufacture finest quality range of Epoxy Paint, Polyurethane Paint, Enamels, and anti-corrosive primers. We are equipped with paint manufacturing facilities with R&D laboratory. The R&D process comprises applied chemical research, quality assurance, new product development, technical support to production and customers. The entire range is highly appreciated for its features like purity, accurate composition, effectiveness, precise pH value, and long shelf life.

Our devotion to supply quality products, maintain quality management system, and provide quality service has helped us in achieving ISO 9001: 2015 certification.

INORCO is a regular supplier of organic solvents to flexible packaging companies, auto components manufacturer, ink divisions, and heat treatment units across the Northern region. INORCO coatings are used in epoxy flooring, finishing lines of major crane manufacturers, and allied machine equipment’s for corrosion protection properties. INORCO has recently ventured into coatings conforming to I.S & defence specifications and is adept to manufacture infra-red reflective coatings for camouflage properties.
